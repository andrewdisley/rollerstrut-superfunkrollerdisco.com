Superfunk Roller Disco and party night at Liquid, Oldham

Friday 02 December 2011

for Straight Singles 20+ years old

Join us for a Superfunk Roller disco and party night in Manchester

Are you ready for a little Roller Disco Romance?

Superfunk Roller Disco are proud to lead the revival of a cult fun activity from the 70s and 80s. This Singles Only event promises to be a fantastic and fun event!

This night puts the fun back into going out by being at the fore of a roller skating and disco revival, encouraging dressing up, general silliness and dancing your socks off.

Of course, you can’t have a Roller Disco without disco, so there’ll be a few disc jockeys on hand to keep you err, rolling!

Skate Safe

Superfunk Roller Disco promote responsible drinking and recommend the use of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) which is available when you hire skates, at no extra charge.

Wrist protection will be MANDATORY at this event - we will have wrist protection for everybody to borrow FREE OF CHARGE on the night.

All skaters are required to read and complete the Roller Rules and Health & Safety Form prior to skating on the roller disco floor.
